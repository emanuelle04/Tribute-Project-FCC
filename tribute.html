Biography

Ariano Vilar Suassuna, lawyer, teacher, theatologist and novelist.

Ariano Suassuna was a writer and Brazilian playwright, author of the Auto da Compadecida, considered his masterpiece that was adapted for cinema and television. In addition to being a renowned writer and one of the greatest in Brazil, Ariano was a teacher and idealizer of the Armorial Movement that valorized the popular arts. In this movement, the artists had the intention of creating an erudite art from elements of the popular culture of Northeast Brazil.

In a soft voice, filled with humorous phrases, the writer and poet flew far beyond the backlands of Cariri, where he spent his childhood. At age 86, the enthusiasm with which he leads his life does not fail to live up to the title of immortal, granted by the Brazilian Academy of Letters. I say that his literature has universal aspects. From a village, the Ariano managed to conquer the world

- Elias Sabbag
